## About MediKeep

MediKeep is a personal health record management system that allows you to:

- **Track Medical Records**: Store and organize all your health information in one place
- **Manage Multiple Patients**: Support for managing records for family members
- **Attach Documents**: Store lab reports, imaging results, and medical documents
- **Share with Caregivers**: Invite others to view patient records
- **Export Data**: Generate reports and export your medical history
